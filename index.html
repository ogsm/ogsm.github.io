I am a PhD candidate at The Australian National University. I study the physics of shallow earth in the Research School of Earth Sciences. Currently, I am developing an intensive laboratory understanding for seismic signature of fluids in cracked media. I do this with broadband investigation of seismic properties of dry and fluid saturated real and proxy crustal materials – such as rocks, sintered beads and cracked glass.
